Frequently Asked Questions

What types of desserts do you offer?

We offer a delightful range of desserts including cakes, cookies, brownies, pastries, and specialty treats. Our menu features both classic favorites and imaginative creations.

Do you offer custom cakes or desserts?

Yes, we specialize in custom orders! Whether it’s a unique cake design or personalized cookies, we’re happy to work with you to create something special for your occasion.

Are your desserts available for delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Memphis. We also provide curbside pickup for your convenience.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies?

We strive to accommodate various dietary needs. Please let us know about any allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order, and we’ll do our best to provide suitable options.

Can I place an order for a large event?

Absolutely! We handle orders for weddings, parties, corporate events, and more. Please contact us in advance to discuss your event and ensure we can meet your needs.

What are your store hours?

On Monday to Friday, we are open 7 AM - 6 PM. Saturday's are 8 AM - 5 PM and Sundays are 10 AM - 4 PM. For any changes or holiday hours, please check our website or give us a call.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer a selection of vegan and gluten-free desserts. Please check our menu or contact us for specific options and availability.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by phone or by visiting our store in person. For custom orders, it’s best to contact us directly to discuss details. Our phone number is 555-678-9012

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or modify an order, please let us know as soon as possible. Our cancellation policy varies depending on the order type and timing, so please review our policy on our website or contact us for more information.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased online or at our store. They make a perfect gift for any dessert lover!
